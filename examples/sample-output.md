# 🍜 Authentic Sichuan in Manhattan / 曼哈顿正宗川菜

Found **3 high-confidence picks**. 按 authenticity 排序. 最强信号是 #1 —— 厨子 migration 的故事.

## #1 Chili (传说中的"川菜暗号店")
**Midtown East · $$–$$$** · 川菜 Sichuan
**Authenticity: 9/10** · 信号: ex-Café China 厨子 migration · 中文 reviewer 比例高 · "Michelin-star-level prep" 出现在多个 review

The insider pick of the moment. 社区 consensus 是 —— 当 Café China 在 2020 丢掉 Michelin star 之后，**多个原 Café China 后厨老师傅 moved over to Chili**, 现在 Chili 已经在 川菜 quality 上 surpass 了 Café China. Less PR, more 麻辣 honesty.

**必点 must order:**
- 🌶️ 麻婆豆腐 Mapo Tofu — silky, 真·麻辣, 没被 Midtown 化掉
- 🥩 孜然羊肉 Cumin Lamb — Reddit 反复点名, 香料 layering 到位
- 🥢 担担面 Dan Dan Noodles — 芝麻 + chili oil ratio 拿捏得准
- 🌶️ 红油抄手 Spicy Wontons — house chili oil 是亮点
- 🥩 回锅肉 Twice-Cooked Pork — 肥瘦比 + 豆瓣酱发酵感都对

📍 Midtown East · 🔗 [Starved.io 综合 Reddit/IG](https://starved.io/nyc/restaurant/50159689/chili)

## #2 Szechuan Mountain House / 川山甲
**East Village (23 St Marks Pl) · also Midtown · $$–$$$** · 川菜 Sichuan
**Authenticity: 9/10** · 信号: "refuses broad fusion trends" · "deepens a single regional voice" · "they do not play around with spice"

NY 川菜 institution. The Infatuation 原话: *"refuses broad fusion trends and instead deepens a single regional voice"* —— 这种 framing 在 review 里出现就是绿灯. 起家在 Flushing (NYC 真·中餐 zone), 扩到 Manhattan 之后没有 dilute 辣度去迎合 Midtown 客流.

**必点 must order:**
- 🌶️ 麻婆豆腐 Mapo Tofu — "extra black pepper kick on top of peppercorn + chili base", 有人说 "perfection"
- 🌶️ 辣子鸡 La Zi Ji — crispy 鸡块 tossed with **a small mountain of dried red chilis + peppercorns**
- 🍲 毛血旺 Mao Xue Wang — pickled-spicy 大杂烩, 川菜深度菜, 不是入门款

📍 23 St Marks Pl, East Village (也有 Midtown 店) · 🔗 [The Infatuation review](https://www.theinfatuation.com/new-york/reviews/szechuan-mountain-house)

## #3 Mountain House (Hell's Kitchen)
**353 W 46th St · $$** · 川菜 Sichuan
**Authenticity: 8/10** · 信号: 注意 — 这家**不是** #2! 是另一家独立运营 · Reddit 反复推 · "spicy, numbing, well balanced"

⚠️ 名字非常容易跟 Szechuan Mountain House 搞混 (而且 Yelp 上也叫 "Mountain House") —— 但这家在 W 46th, Hell's Kitchen, **是不同 owner 不同店**. Reddit 上多次出现 "one of my favorite Sichuan spots in NYC, everything we ordered hit." 比 #2 更安静、更本地, 4.7 Google rating.

**必点 must order:**
- 🌶️ 麻婆豆腐 Mapo Tofu — "great, spicy, numbing, just really well balanced" (Reddit 原话)
- 🐟 水煮鱼 Shui Zhu Yu — boiled fish 浮在 chili oil 海里, 川菜核心招牌
- 🌿 干煸四季豆 Dry-Fried Green Beans — 锅气足的话这道是 baseline test

📍 353 W 46th St, Hell's Kitchen · 🔗 [Yelp](https://www.yelp.com/biz/mountain-house-new-york)

## 🚫 我故意没放的 / Deliberately skipped

| 餐厅 | 为什么 skip |
|---|---|
| **Café China** (Midtown) | 中文 customer 比例不错, 但多个 review flag *"not as spicy as Sichuan enthusiasts want"* — 为 Midtown 客流 dial down 了辣度. 现在原班厨子也走了. Authenticity 7/10, 适合 ambience, 不适合 麻辣 purity. |
| **Han Dynasty** (East Village + 多家分店) | "Very busy, limited menu repetition" 出现在 review 里 = mainstream chain feel, 不是 hidden-gem 选项. 不难吃, 但不会让 Sichuan 老饕惊艳. |
| **Sky Pavilion** (Hell's Kitchen) | The Infatuation 自己说: *"a great place to bring peppercorn first-timers"* + 菜单上还有 dim sum + 美式中餐. Crowd-pleaser positioning, 不是 insider pick. |
| **Spicy Moon** (East Village) | Vegan Sichuan, 自身做得不错, 但 vegan adaptation 本身就压低 authenticity score. 如果是 vegan 优先选它, 否则不上榜. |

**Cross-checked across:** Infatuation 文章 (3 篇) · Yelp (4 listings) · Tripadvisor (3 listings) · Starved.io · Time Out NY · multiple food blogs.

**Authenticity 怎么打分:**
- ✅ Native-language / 中文 reviewer 占比
- ✅ "refuses fusion" / "deepens regional voice" 信号
- ✅ Chef provenance story (Chili 的 ex-Café China 迁移)
- ✅ "do not play around with spice" / "first-timer-friendly" 取反 (后者拉低 score)
- ✅ 多源交叉验证 (≥2 source 才进榜)

**⚠️ Methodology caveat:** Reddit 的 robots.txt 在 2024 年改了, 现在 Google index 抓不到 r/FoodNYC, r/AsianEats, r/AskSF, r/sgv 这些子版的内容, `site:reddit.com/r/<sub>` 搜索基本 0 result. 已经在 SKILL.md 里把 Reddit `site:` 搜索从 menu 移除. 替代源: starved.io 第三方 aggregator, 或者直接 reddit.com 站内搜.
